The shop

The Bay Area's premier archery range.

Pacifica Archery opened in 1998 and moved to our current Daly City home in 2005. Today we're the place between San Francisco and the mid-Peninsula where you can walk in, get fitted, rent or buy a bow, take a lesson, and shoot indoors — rain, wind, or fog, year-round, under one roof.

Full-service means full-service: 15 lanes at 20 yards, in-house tuning and repair, leagues, the JOAD youth program, group parties, and a pro shop stocked for longbow, recurve, and compound shooters alike.

The people

The staff you'll meet

Our customers don't just review the range — they name the staff. Patient, knowledgeable, and genuinely happy you're here.

Beth Snell

Co-owner

Didn't know anything about archery when Joe convinced her to start the business. She went to Mathews and PSE archery schools to learn, but found that experiencing the day-to-day operations was her best teacher. She enjoyed both competitive shooting and hunting. As she ages she has slowed down on both, but still loves teaching and interacting with customers — and doesn't think she'll ever retire, wanting to be around as long as possible.

Nathan Tamayo

On-site Manager

Has been involved with his family's business since his youth. From fletching arrows to teaching new archers, he's done it all. He learned everything he could about archery from his father, and as much as possible about business management from his mother. As Pacifica Archery's on-site manager, he continues striving to bring out the best in the staff.

Eric Ashton

Level 2 Instructor

A part-time Level 2 instructor who's prepping to move into a career in an electrician's union. Eric enjoys informing others about the sport as well as creating bonds with new and returning archers. He grew up in the family business but started working regularly at Pacifica Archery in 2017, and has since been commended on his instructional capabilities as well as his ability to interact with others.

Christ Estoque

Staff

Started his love for archery after taking a class at Community College of San Francisco. After serving the public with San Francisco Parks and Recreation, he's come to Pacifica Archery. He also enjoys anime, manga, and whiskey tasting.

Gabi Mello

Staff

Started her time with Pacifica Archery as a student during our Youth Archery Program and sought employment so she could help other teens grow in the sport. She is studying cinema and enjoys going to local music shows.

For everyone

A sport that looks like the Bay Area.

Daly City is one of the most diverse cities in America, and our range reflects it. Archery has deep roots across cultures — and elite tradition in the Korean and Chinese communities right here in our neighborhood. Every age, every background, every skill level is welcome on our lanes.
