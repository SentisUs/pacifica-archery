Good questions

First-timer FAQ

Do I need my own equipment?

Nope. Every beginner package includes a bow, arrows, and all safety gear. Just show up in comfortable clothes.

How old do I have to be?

All archers must be 8 years or older. There's no upper limit — we regularly coach archers well into their 70s and 80s.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes. Avoid loose sleeves or hoodie drawstrings near your chest, and tie back long hair so nothing catches the bowstring.

Is it safe?

Very. Our indoor range is fully supervised, every session starts with safety rules, and our coaches stay close. Archery has one of the lowest injury rates of any sport.

Do I need to book ahead?

We recommend booking online, especially for weekend slots. Weeknight and Midweek Hunter Hours times are the easiest to walk into — and the cheapest.

Can I just watch first?

Of course. Come by, see the range, talk to the staff. But fair warning — most people who come to watch end up shooting.
