FREE PARKING

No meters. No garages. No circling the block.

Pull right up, grab your bow, and shoot.

Why drive into the city to fight for parking?

Pacifica Archery sits right off I-280 / Junipero Serra Blvd with a big, free, on-site lot. It's one of the most underrated reasons our customers keep coming back — getting here is effortless.

  • From San Francisco (north): Take I-280 South to the Daly City / Junipero Serra Blvd exit. We're minutes from the city line.
  • From the Peninsula (south): Take I-280 North to the Daly City exit and follow Junipero Serra Blvd.
  • Wheelchair accessible with easy ground-level entry. Look for the Pacifica Archery sign next to CyBelle's Pizza.
